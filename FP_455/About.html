About This Map

Purpose

This web map visualizes hiking trails in Great Smoky Mountains National Park, categorized by difficulty (easy, moderate, hard) (there will be more layers to mention here once I add them). The goal is to help users understand trail accessibility and terrain variation across the park.

Study Area

The study area is Great Smoky Mountains National Park, located along the border of Tennessee and North Carolina.

Data Sources

  • Trail data: National Park Service Open Data
  • Basemaps: OpenStreetMap and USGS
  • Processing: ArcGIS Pro

Map Features

  • Trail classification by difficulty (more layers coming soon)
  • Interactive popups with trail attributes
  • Layer toggling
  • Multiple basemap options

How to Use

Users can toggle trail layers using the legend, click on trails to view attributes, and switch basemaps for different visual perspectives of the terrain.
